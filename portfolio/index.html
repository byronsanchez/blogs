Target Platforms

Tablets

Reach a wider audience with development and design for the growing tablet market. Applications are designed with varying tablet sizes and screen orientations in mind.

Phones

Mobile devices offer many differing features across platforms. All applications are designed for a seamless experience across platforms while taking advantage of the unique features each platform offers.

The Web

Taking advantage of the web as a platform for applications opens them up to a global audience. The application becomes accessible from all devices and engages a larger and larger amount of users.
