This privacy policy covers how hackBytes.com will treat all information received from visitors. The policy is really simple.

The only personal information this site may have on you is anything you voluntarily disclose.

This information is kept private. It is not shared with anyone.

If I make any changes to the privacy policy, the changes will be published here.
